Development of an Energy Monitoring and Regulating System to Reduce Building Energy Consumption

Thesis Project (2024)

A comprehensive energy monitoring and control system designed to optimize building energy consumption through real-time monitoring, data analysis, and automated control mechanisms. The system integrates IoT technology with power electronics to create an intelligent energy management solution that helps reduce energy waste and improve building efficiency.

View Report

Automatic Room Light Intensity Control with Respect to Natural Light Intensity

3rd Year Project (2023)

Automatically adjusting the level of the room's lighting Using an Arduino, a light sensor, and a dimming module, a home's or office's lighting system may be automatically increased and decreased. Such automatic room light control with respect to natural light can be applied in many places, such as classrooms, faculty quarters, garages, stairwells, toilets, study rooms, etc., where illumination isn't required to be constant but only when natural light intensity is low.
